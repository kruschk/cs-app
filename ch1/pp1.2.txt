My solution:
P_new = I*P_old
P_new = (1 - a)P_old + a*P_old*k
P_new = P_old*(1 - a + a*k)
I = P_new/P_old = (1 - a + a*k)
I + a - 1 = a*k
k = (I + a - 1)/a
k = (4 + 0.9 - 1)/0.9 = 4.33...
