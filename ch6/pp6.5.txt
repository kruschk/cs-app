Note: the numbers in the solution have changed, but this approach
appears to be correct.

A.

t = 128*10^15 B / 470 MB/s
  = 128*10^15 B / 470*10^6 B/s
  = ((128/470)*10^9 s) * (1 h/3600 s) * (1 d/24 h) * (1 a/365 d)
  ~= 8.64 a

B.

t = 128*10^15 B / 303 MB/s
  = 128*10^15 B / 303*10^6 B/s
  = ((128/303)*10^9 s) * (1 h/3600 s) * (1 d/24 h) * (1 a/365 d)
  ~= 13.4 a

C.

t = 128*10^15 B / 20 GB/d
  = 128*10^15 B / 20*10^9 B/d
  = ((128/20)*10^6 d) * (1 a/365 d)
  ~= 17534 a
