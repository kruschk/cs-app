A.
0.1 is expressed exactly in binary as:
0.1 = 0b0.000110011[0011]...

We can express this as:
1.1001 1001 1001 1001 1001 100[1 001]... * 2^-4

which is approximately:
1.1001 1001 1001 1001 1001 100 * 2^-4
  ^--------------------------^     ^^
               f                   E

Matching this form to the values defined by IEEE 754 single-precision floats:
f = [0001 1001 1001 1001 1001 100]
E = -4
where
E = e - bias -> e = E + bias
bias = 2^(k - 1) - 1
     = 2^(8 - 1) - 1
     = 2^7 - 1
     = 128 - 1
     = 127
e = -4 + 127
  = 123
  = 0b0111 1011

123 = 2*61 + 1
 61 = 2*30 + 1
 30 = 2*15 + 0
 15 = 2*7  + 1
  7 = 2*3  + 1
  3 = 2*1  + 1
  1 = 2*0  + 1
