A.
0.1 - x = 0.1 - 0b0.00011001100110011001100
        = 0b0.000110011[0011]... - 0b0.00011001100110011001100

  0.00011001100110011001100110011[0011]...
- 0.00011001100110011001100000000.........
------------------------------------------
  0.00000000000000000000000110011[0011]...

B.
0b0.00000000000000000000000110011[0011]... = 2^-24 + 2^-25 + 2^-28 + 2^-29 + ...
                                           = 0.000000095 + ...
                                           ~= 0.000000095

C.
t_actual = 100 hr * 60 min/hr * 60 s/min
         = 360,000 s
count = 360,000 s / 0.1 s
      = 3,600,000
delta_t ~= 0.000000095 s * 3,600,000
        = 0.341981649 s

D.
d = v*delta_t
  = 2,000 m/s * 0.341981649 s
  = 683.963298798 m
