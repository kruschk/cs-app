Bits     | Value | Bits     | Value

011 0000 |     1 | 0111 000 |     1
101 1110 |  15/2 | 1001 111 |  15/2
010 1001 | 25/32 | 0110 100 |   3/4
110 1111 |  31/2 | 1011 000 |    16
000 0001 |  1/64 | 0001 000 |  1/64


101 1110:
2^(5 - 3)*(1 + 7/8) = 2^2*15/8 = 15/2 = 0b111.1 = 0b1.111*2^2
e = E + bias = 2 + 7 = 9
1001 111
2^(9 - 7)*(1 + 7/8) = 4*15/8 = 15/2

010 1001:
2^(2 - 3)*(1 + 9/16) = 1/2*25/16 = 25/32 = 0b0.1 1001 = 0b1.1001*2^-1
                     ~= 0b1.100*2^-1 (round down)
e = E + bias = -1 + 7 = 6
0110 100
2^(6 - 7)*(1 + 1/2) = 1/2*3/2 = 3/4

110 1111:
2^(6 - 3)*(1 + 15/16) = 8*31/16 = 31/2 = 0b1111.1 = 0b1.1111*2^3
                      ~= 0b10.000*2^3 (round up)
                      = 0b1.000*2^4 
e = E + bias = 4 + 7 = 11
1011 000
2^(11 - 7)*(1 + 0) = 2^4 = 16

000 0001:
2^(1 - 3)*1/16 = 1/4*1/16 = 1/64 = 2^-6 = 0b0.000001 = 1.000*2^-6
e = E + bias = -6 + 7 = 1
0001 000
2^(1 - 7)*(1 + 0) = 2^-6*1 = 1/64
