The number of distinct addresses is equal to the page size times the
number of page table entries (PTEs). Expressed algebraically, we have:

N = P * PTE

Solving for PTE yields:

PTE = N / P
    = 2^n / 2^p
    = 2^(n - p)

The rest is just computation.

 n | P = 2^p | Number of PTEs
---+---------+-----------------------
12 |     1 K | 2^(12 - 10) = 2^2 =  4
16 |    16 K | 2^(16 - 14) = 2^2 =  4
24 |     2 M | 2^(24 - 21) = 2^3 =  8
36 |     1 G | 2^(36 - 30) = 2^6 = 64
